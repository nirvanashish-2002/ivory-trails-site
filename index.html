Our Story

A Legacy Reimagined

Nature is not a backdrop here.
It is a teacher, a healer,
and a shared home.

Most travel keeps nature at arm's length — observed through glass, packaged for comfort, forgotten by Sunday. We built something different.

The Ivory Trails sits at the edge of Mudumalai Tiger Reserve, where the forest doesn't apologise for being itself. This is a place where people don't merely visit nature — they learn to live alongside it. To cohabit, to observe, to move at its pace.

Formerly Wild Breeze Resort, this land was always rooted in the wilderness around it. The name has changed. The spirit has deepened.

Our logo tells the story: an elephant and a tree — two rooted things, coexisting. Power and grace. Strength and stillness. That is the foundation this place is built on.

Come Find Us

Intentional Hospitality

Not mass tourism. Every decision here is shaped by one question: does this serve the forest as much as it serves the guest? This is hospitality with a conscience.

Coexistence by Design

Fourteen rooms. Ancient trees. Wildlife trails that predate the resort. The animals didn't move for us — we built around them. That choice defines everything about this place.

Wellness as a Way of Being

No curated programmes. No itinerary. Forest walks at dawn, birdsong at breakfast, starlight after dinner. The forest does the work. You just have to show up.

FAQ

Got Questions?

Find answers to the most common questions about rooms, facilities, and how to get to the resort.

  • Check-in is from 1:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Guests are requested not to arrive or leave the property post 7:00 PM due to the rules of the forest authorities.

  • The Ivory Trails is located in Masinagudi, Tamil Nadu, and is easily accessible by road. The nearest airport is Coimbatore International Airport (approx. 120 km), from where you can hire a taxi or arrange a transfer. The nearest railway station is Mysore Junction (approx. 90 km). The resort is well-connected via road from Ooty, Mysore, and Bangalore.

  • The resort has 14 rooms, ensuring a quiet and uncrowded experience. Most rooms accommodate up to 2–3 guests, with the option of an extra bed.

  • Daily breakfast is included in all room rates, along with in-room tea and coffee. Additional meal plans are available — please check while booking or contact us for details.

  • Yes. The resort sits close to the Masinagudi safari zones within Mudumalai Tiger Reserve. We can assist with jungle safaris, nature walks, and local exploration — simply reach out before or during your stay.
